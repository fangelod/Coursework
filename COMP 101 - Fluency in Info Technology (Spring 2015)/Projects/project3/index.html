The Philippines

What is it?

The Philippines is a country that consists of 7,107 islands located in Southeast Asia. It has three geographical divisions: Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. Luzon, indicated by the region in blue in the image to the left, makes up the northern majority of the country. The country's capital, Manila, resides in this geographical region. Visayas, indicated by the yellow, makes up the the middle group of islands of the country. Mindanao, indicated by the red makes up the southern mainland and it's surrounding islands. Just southwest of the Philippines resides the country Malaysia.

Language

The national language of the Philippines is Filipino, however English is also an official language. The language of Filipino has many different dialects that differs by region. The most prominent language in the country is Tagalog. This dialect, along with English, are the languages used taught in school. However, depending on the region, schools may incorporate the regional dialect into the lessons as well.
