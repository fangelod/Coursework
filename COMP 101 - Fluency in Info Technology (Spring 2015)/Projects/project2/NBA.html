Basketball at the Professional Level

What is the "NBA"?

The NBA stands for the National Basketball Association. It is the collection of teams the play basketball at the professional level in the United States. It is considered one of the top leagues in the world. Every year, the buzz surrounds the NBA finals, which are the playoffs for the league. Every NBA player's goal is to win as many NBA championships as possible. Basketball is the best professional sport to play in America, as NBA players are the world's best paid sportmen by average annual salary per player.

Rules and Regulations

Basketball's rules change between the different associations. In the NBA, a few rules are different from basketball played at the collegiate level.

  • Total Time: Four 12 minute quarters
  • Shot Clock: 24 seconds
  • 3 Point Line: 19'9"
  • Width of Paint: 16 ft
  • Fouls: 6 personal fouls until fouling out
  • Possessions: Jump ball
